Welcome to Marketing Fridays

Marketing Fridays was launched in April 2018, to address a unique need for marketing professionals to learn from one another and get an opportunity to network. The reality is that there are not a lot of platforms for us to share ideas and just get to know each other within the industry. There has been a few movements that have really progressed over the last few years, but we need as many platforms and voices advocating for Marketing excellence and for us to pay if forward as we broaden access to Marketing.

Hence on the first Friday of every month, Marketing Fridays invites you to join other practicing marketing professionals in Johannesburg for coffee, light breakfast and an insightful special guest address in a setting that affords you a chance to establish invaluable contact with other marketing professionals and enhance your understanding on Marketing.
